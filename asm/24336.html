5F10: Background address buffer.
Buffer that holds the currently processed background data depending on the background reference value.
BACKGROUND_REFERENCE Background Data
$01 BACKGROUND_1_ADDRESSES
$02 BACKGROUND_2_ADDRESSES
$03 BACKGROUND_3_ADDRESSES
Populated from one of 5F53, 5F62 or 5F71.
