8BE8: Player 2 redefined keys.
When player 2 is using the keyboard, these byte pairs will be populated with the corresponding keys.
MSB LSB
Port Bits
See KEY_DATA.
PLAYER_2_UP 8BE8 DEFB $00,$00 Player 2 - "up" key.
PLAYER_2_UP_RIGHT 8BEA DEFB $00,$00 Player 2 - "up/ right" key.
PLAYER_2_RIGHT 8BEC DEFB $00,$00 Player 2 - "right" key.
PLAYER_2_DOWN_RIGHT 8BEE DEFB $00,$00 Player 2 - "down/ right" key.
PLAYER_2_DOWN 8BF0 DEFB $00,$00 Player 2 - "down" key.
PLAYER_2_DOWN_LEFT 8BF2 DEFB $00,$00 Player 2 - "down/ left" key.
PLAYER_2_LEFT 8BF4 DEFB $00,$00 Player 2 - "left" key.
PLAYER_2_UP_LEFT 8BF6 DEFB $00,$00 Player 2 - "up/ left" key.
PLAYER_2_FIRE 8BF8 DEFB $00,$00 Player 2 - "fire" key.
